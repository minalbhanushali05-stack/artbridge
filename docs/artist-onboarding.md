# Artist Onboarding Flow

## Phase 1: Registration

### WhatsApp Initiation
Artist sends "Hi" to WhatsApp Business number

```
Bot: "Namaste! 🎨 Welcome to ArtBridge. We help you sell your art globally.

How would you like to proceed?
1. Register as artist
2. Learn more"
```

### Information Collection
Bot collects in artist's preferred language:
- Name
- Phone number (verification)
- Region/City
- Art form (e.g., Madhubani, Warli, Gond)
- Preferred language
- Bank/UPI details

### Verification
- OTP sent to phone number
- Artist verifies OTP
- Profile activated

## Phase 2: Photo & Product Upload

### Photo Submission
Artist sends 3-5 photos of artwork via WhatsApp
- AI validates image quality
- Checks for multiple angles
- Confirms authenticity

### AI Processing
**Google Gemini generates:**
1. Product title
2. Description (150-200 words)
3. Cultural/regional context
4. Suggested pricing
5. Art form classification
6. Search tags

### Pricing Intelligence
- AI analyzes similar products
- Suggests market-competitive price
- Artist can accept or modify

## Phase 3: Storytelling

### Story Generation
AI creates narrative including:
- Artist background
- Art form history
- Traditional techniques
- Symbolism & meaning
- Regional significance

### QR Code
Unique QR code generated for each product:
- Links to artist profile
- Shows all artist's works
- Displays product story
- Optional: Video of artist creating work

## Phase 4: Listing Launch

### Product Goes Live
- Added to storefront
- Indexed for search
- Featured in "New Arrivals"
- Artist notified via WhatsApp

### Real-time Updates
Artist receives WhatsApp notifications:
- "Your artwork is live!"
- Product views
- New orders
- Shipment tracking
- Customer reviews

## Phase 5: Order Management

### Order Notification
```
Artist receives:
- "🎉 New order! 1 × [Product Name]
- Total: ₹X
- Shipping to: [Country]
- 📦 Pickup details: [Link]
- 💰 Payment: Received"
```

### Fulfillment
1. Artist packs artwork
2. Follows quality guidelines
3. Includes QR code sticker
4. Drops at India Post
5. Updates tracking

### Payment Processing
- Automatic after 3 days (delivery window)
- 80% to artist account
- 20% platform commission
- Real-time WhatsApp notification
- Monthly payout summary
